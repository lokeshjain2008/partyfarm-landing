Add these Google Fonts to your project:

1. Baloo 2 (for headings): https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Baloo+2
2. Inter (for body): https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter

To use, add the following to your <head> in _app.tsx or _document.tsx:
<link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Baloo+2:wght@700&family=Inter:wght@400;700&display=swap" rel="stylesheet" />
